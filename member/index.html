Membership

The start of your professional career

PSPE has callouts every fall and spring for new members. Becoming a member is easy! Just attend the fall or spring callout (online for fall 2020 due to COVID-19), fill out the online application form and return it with your semester or yearly dues. You can see the application form on the right side of the page under notices. PSPE has a general membership meeting every other week, usually featuring a guest speaker and as always free food!*

If you would like the opportunity to meet like-minded individuals and become further involved in PSPE, look for the committee callout dates after the first callout of the semester. Separate committees allow PSPE to host various events throughout the year, and give back to our community. Involvement in a committee is an excellent way to gain valuable leadership experience and learn what it really means to become a professional engineer.

* Specifics of meetings during fall 2020 are still being determined due to required changes as a result of COVID-19. Free food and other normal meeting traditions may depend on a meeting's type of attendance (in-person or otherwise).
