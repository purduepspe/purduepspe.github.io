Thank You!

Through donation of their resources, both corporate and private partners contribute to educational progress of students in groups like the Purdue Society of Professional Engineers. Not only do these resources aid in helping students to learn and grow, but they also play a role in fueling the development of tomorrow's leaders. As a small thank you to our friends, this page serves to acknowledge their generosity.

What We Offer Our Sponsors

Like most relationships in the professional world, there should be benefits for all parties involved. In keeping with this practice we offer all of our PSPE sponsors the opportunity to gain increased visibility within the Purdue community. The name of each of our sponsors will be placed on the PSPE website, and those that designate Research Roundtable or the Fundamentals of Engineering Exam as the destination for their funds will also receive further benefits as listed below.

Fundamentals of Engineering Exam Sponsorship

PSPE is looking for a company to provide a scholarship to those taking the Fundamentals of Engineering exam, which is normally taken by seniors in their last semester of college. This exam provides a measure of student proficiency and is a first step to becoming a professional engineer.

Students interested in receiving a scholarship for the fees associated with the exam would fill out an application, including their personal information, responses to questions (like why they want to become a professional engineer), and a resume for you to consider. Companies that have donated to this cause will be allowed to choose the top students, which will receive a scholarship to pay for exam fees.
