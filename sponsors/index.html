Our Corporate Sponsors

Funding The Future of Engineering Through Philanthropy

Through donation of their resources, industry partners contribute to educational progress of students in groups like the Purdue Society of Professional Engineers. Not only do these resources aid in helping students to learn and grow, but they also play a role in fueling the development of tomorrow's leaders. As a small thank you to our friends in industry, this page serves to acknowledge their generosity.

What We Offer Our Sponsors

Like most relationships in the professional world, there should be benefits for all parties involved. In keeping with this practice we offer our PSPE corporate sponsors the opportunity to gain increased visibility within the Purdue community. Not only do our sponsors gain a place for their company logo on this webpage, but this year we are also offering logo placement at both our Research Roundtable and Professional Engineering FE exam events.
